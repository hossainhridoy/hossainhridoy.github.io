About Me

Hossain Hridoy is a visionary teenage animation YouTuber, digital creator, and developer from Bangladesh. He specializes in creative storytelling through mobile-based 2D animation, driving the digital frontier with Lembux Group. Starting his journey in 2023, Hossain has created engaging content for kids and families, focusing on fun stories, horror cartoons, and educational animations. In addition to animation, he develops web applications like IPTV players, showcasing his skills in modern web technologies. Passionate about technology and creativity, Hossain aims to inspire young creators in Bangladesh and beyond.
